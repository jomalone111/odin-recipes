Cheesecake

Photo by Aliona Gumeniuk on Unsplash

Description

This is a family favorite. A buttery base of Maria cookies combined with a creamy vanilla filling creates an irresitable combination. What puts this cheesecake over the top is the tangy sweet dulce de leche topping. Make it once, and you will never make another cheesecake again.

This recipe was adapted from Pati Jinich's Dulce de Leche Cheesecake

Ingredients:

Crust

  • 1.5 cups ground Maria Cookies
  • 6 Tablespoons butter

Filling

  • 1 pound of cream cheese
  • 14oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Topping

  • 1.5 cups sour cream
  • 14oz dulce de leche

To Prepare:

  1. Make Crust:
    • Combine Ground cookies and butter until throughly mixed
    • Put mixture into springform pan
    • Pat mixture evenly around pan, pushing it up the sides
    • Refrigerate
  2. Make Filling:
    • Place cream cheese in bowl of stand mixer and beat until smooth. About 4 minutes
    • Add sweetened condense milk and beat until combined. Scrape bowl as needed
    • Add eggs one at a time, combining completely before the next addition
    • Set aside
  3. Make Topping:
    • In a seperate bowl, mix sour cream and dulce de leche until combined
  4. Make Cheesecakse:
    • Preheat Oven to 350f
    • Remove sprinform pan from refrigerator.
    • Spread cream cheese filling into pan
    • Place cheesecake in oven and bake for 35 minutes
    • Remove from oven and cool for 10 minutes
    • Add dulce de leche topping to cooled cheesecake
    • Place cheescake back in the over for 10 more minutes
    • Remove from oven and let cool completely before removing from the pan
